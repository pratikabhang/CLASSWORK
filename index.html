Formula: GPA = Total Grade Points / Total Credits

Example Calculation:

Course 1: 3 credits, Grade: O (6)
Course 2: 4 credits, Grade: B (4)
Course 3: 2 credits, Grade: C (3)

Total Grade Points = (3×6) + (4×4) + (2×3) = 18 + 16 + 6 = 40

Total Credits = 3 + 4 + 2 = 9

GPA = 40 / 9 ≈ 4.44
